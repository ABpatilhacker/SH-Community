Guidelines

Community Rules

Our standards ensure SH Community remains a premium, safe, and valuable space for all members.

  • 01
    Respect & Professionalism — Treat every member with respect. Zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination.
  • 02
    No Spam — Self-promotion must be approved. Unsolicited DMs are strictly forbidden.
  • 03
    Confidentiality — What happens in SH Community stays here. Do not share private discussions externally.
  • 04
    Active Participation — Members who are inactive for 60+ days may be removed to maintain community quality.
  • 05
    Quality Over Quantity — Share valuable, meaningful content. No low-effort posts or irrelevant links.
